Happy

Family

We at Carriesoft believe Happiness begins at home. It is our mission to make sure all the contents we make is family-oriented, funny, cheerful, active, educational, and healthy always.

We'r Kids Content Master

We create and publish contents for kids and family, providing them everyday entertainment from short video clips, 3D animation, web drama to on-stage musical performances.

Mobile And TV

Our video clips and animations are serviced on our channels on new media platforms like youtube.com. We also provide our contents to IPTVs, Cable TVs, OTT services, and smart TVs.

Online And Offline

Based on our content IP, our business to meet kids and their family expands to off-line such as indoor playgrounds, musical performances and education centers.

Play And Learn

Our priority is to make our contents fun and healthy for kids in the world. We believe children learn and grow through positive playing experiences.

What We DO

Media Service

We own a TV station, CarrieTV, which broadcasts all of our video contents to IPTV, OTT, and Cable TV service providers.

Mobile Game

We publish mobile games, specialized to kids and family users, worldwide both in Android and iOS markets.

Brand And Character Licensing

“Carrie and Friends” are well-known kids-friendly characters in Asian countries. There are hundreds licensed items in the market from toys to food-and-beverage.

Musical Perfomance

We create, present and tour a series of a family musical every year. The story, dance, lyrics and music are specially created to entertain children and their family.

Playground

We has built an indoor play area called “Carrie Kids Café” where pre-school children explore various toys, ride trampoline, play board games and even get beauty services such as hair decoration.

Child Education

As we believe children learn as they play, we provide various classes through videos, animations and books. “Hello Carrie English” is an English teaching and learning program for kids. “Hello Carrie Korean Alphabet”, “Hello Carrie Ballet” etc. We provide well-made educational programs for kids.
